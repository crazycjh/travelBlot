# 琉森 (Lucerne)

## **歷史 (History)**
琉森的歷史可追溯至公元8世紀，最初是一個修道院的所在地。到1198年，琉森已發展為重要的市場鎮，並逐漸成為連接意大利和德國的貿易要道。1332年，琉森加入了瑞士邦聯，成為聯邦早期的成員之一。在16世紀宗教改革期間，琉森保持天主教傳統，成為瑞士中部的宗教與文化中心。如今，琉森以保存完好的中世紀建築和豐富的歷史遺跡聞名，如卡佩爾廊橋和舊城區。

**Lucerne's history dates back to the 8th century as a monastery settlement. By 1198, it had evolved into an important market town and a vital trade route between Italy and Germany. In 1332, Lucerne joined the Swiss Confederation as an early member. During the Reformation in the 16th century, Lucerne retained its Catholic roots, becoming a religious and cultural hub of central Switzerland. Today, Lucerne is renowned for its well-preserved medieval architecture and historical landmarks like the Chapel Bridge and the Old Town.**

## **地理 (Geography)**
琉森位於瑞士中部，琉森湖（Vierwaldstättersee）北岸，阿爾卑斯山脈環繞其四周。該城市享有溫和的海洋性氣候，夏季涼爽，冬季溫和，是旅遊的理想地點。琉森湖形狀不規則，被認為是瑞士最美的湖泊之一，而城市南部的皮拉圖斯山和瑞吉山則是當地的自然地標。

**Lucerne is located in central Switzerland on the northern shore of Lake Lucerne (Vierwaldstättersee), surrounded by the Alps. The city enjoys a mild maritime climate, with cool summers and mild winters, making it an ideal travel destination. Lake Lucerne, with its irregular shape, is considered one of Switzerland's most beautiful lakes, while Mount Pilatus and Mount Rigi to the south are iconic natural landmarks.**

## **此處特色 (Unique Aspects)**
琉森以其完美結合自然與人文而聞名，城市擁有保存完好的中世紀建築、石板街道和古老的教堂。卡佩爾廊橋（Kapellbrücke）是世界最古老的有頂木橋之一，象徵著琉森的歷史。除此之外，琉森還是音樂與藝術的重鎮，每年舉辦的琉森音樂節吸引世界各地的藝術家和觀眾。

**Lucerne is famous for its seamless blend of nature and culture, with well-preserved medieval architecture, cobblestone streets, and historic churches. The Chapel Bridge (Kapellbrücke) is one of the oldest covered wooden bridges in the world, symbolizing Lucerne's history. Additionally, Lucerne is a hub for music and art, hosting the annual Lucerne Festival that draws artists and audiences from around the globe.**

## **美食 (Cuisine)**
1. **琉森乳酪火鍋（Cheese Fondue）** - 瑞士經典美食，以濃郁的本地乳酪製成。  
2. **瑞士巧克力（Swiss Chocolate）** - 世界聞名的瑞士手工巧克力，品嚐當地特產必不可少。  
3. **瑞士烤馬鈴薯餅（Rösti）** - 金黃色煎土豆餅，是瑞士家常美食的代表。  
4. **琉森湖魚（Lake Lucerne Fish）** - 使用新鮮捕獲的湖魚製作，如鱒魚和白魚。  
5. **蜂蜜蛋糕（Honigkuchen）** - 帶有香料與蜂蜜風味的傳統甜點。

**1. Cheese Fondue - A Swiss classic made with rich local cheese.  
2. Swiss Chocolate - Renowned Swiss handmade chocolates are a must-try.  
3. Rösti - Golden fried potato pancakes, a staple of Swiss cuisine.  
4. Lake Lucerne Fish - Freshly caught fish like trout and whitefish prepared simply to highlight their natural flavors.  
5. Honigkuchen - A traditional spiced honey cake.**

## **著名的觀光景點 (Famous Attractions)**
1. **卡佩爾廊橋（Kapellbrücke）** - 世界上最古老的有頂木橋之一，建於14世紀。  
2. **獅子紀念碑（Lion Monument）** - 一座紀念瑞士傭兵的石雕，被稱為“最悲壯的雕像”。  
3. **皮拉圖斯山（Mount Pilatus）** - 擁有壯麗的景觀和登山路徑。  
4. **瑞吉山（Mount Rigi）** - 被稱為“山峯之后”，以絕美的全景聞名。  
5. **瑞士交通博物館（Swiss Museum of Transport）** - 瑞士最重要的交通博物館之一。  
6. **耶穌會教堂（Jesuit Church）** - 瑞士最重要的巴洛克風格教堂之一。  
7. **舊城區（Old Town）** - 保留中世紀風格的街道和建築。

**1. Chapel Bridge - One of the oldest covered wooden bridges in the world, built in the 14th century.  
2. Lion Monument - A memorial for Swiss mercenaries, often called the "most moving sculpture."  
3. Mount Pilatus - Offers breathtaking views and hiking trails.  
4. Mount Rigi - Known as the "Queen of the Mountains" with stunning panoramic views.  
5. Swiss Museum of Transport - One of Switzerland's most important transport museums.  
6. Jesuit Church - A significant Baroque-style church in Switzerland.  
7. Old Town - Features medieval-style streets and buildings.**

## **健行路線 (Hiking Routes)**
1. **皮拉圖斯山健行（Mount Pilatus Trails）** - 提供多條適合不同難度的路徑。  
2. **瑞吉山健行（Mount Rigi Trails）** - 包含輕鬆至中等難度的步道，適合全家人。  
3. **湖畔漫步路線（Lake Lucerne Promenade）** - 沿湖的悠閒步道，欣賞湖光山色。  
4. **梅吉根峽谷（Meggen Gorge）** - 自然美景與幽靜環境的理想健行地點。

**1. Mount Pilatus Trails - Offers trails of varying difficulty.  
2. Mount Rigi Trails - Includes easy to moderate paths suitable for families.  
3. Lake Lucerne Promenade - A serene lakeside trail with beautiful views.  
4. Meggen Gorge - A perfect hiking spot with natural beauty and tranquility.**


# 鐵力士山 (Mount Titlis)

## **景點名稱 (Attraction Name)**  
鐵力士山（Mount Titlis）

## **歷史資訊 (Historical Information)**  
鐵力士山位於瑞士中部，海拔約3,238公尺，是阿爾卑斯山脈中一個知名的高峰。早在19世紀，鐵力士山就因其壯麗的冰川景觀吸引了探險家和旅行者。1913年，第一條連接山頂的纜車系統建成，隨後不斷升級，讓遊客能輕鬆抵達山頂欣賞冰雪世界。今天，鐵力士山以其360度旋轉纜車（Titlis Rotair）而聞名，這是世界首創的旋轉纜車，提供了獨特的觀景體驗。

**Mount Titlis, located in central Switzerland at an elevation of approximately 3,238 meters, is a prominent peak in the Alps. It has been attracting explorers and travelers since the 19th century due to its stunning glacial landscapes. In 1913, the first cable car system connecting the summit was constructed, and it has since been upgraded to allow visitors easy access to the icy wonderland. Today, Mount Titlis is renowned for its Titlis Rotair, the world's first rotating cable car, offering a unique panoramic experience.**

## **此處特色 (Unique Aspects)**  
鐵力士山最大的特色是其壯觀的冰川景觀和全年皆可進行的冰雪活動，如滑雪和冰川步道。此外，遊客可以探索鐵力士冰川洞穴（Glacier Cave），這是一個天然形成的冰洞，內部冰層散發著神秘的藍光。山上的懸索橋（Titlis Cliff Walk）是歐洲海拔最高的吊橋，提供令人驚心動魄的視野，適合喜歡挑戰自我的遊客。

**Mount Titlis stands out for its magnificent glacier views and year-round snow activities such as skiing and glacier walking. Visitors can explore the Titlis Glacier Cave, a naturally formed ice cavern with mesmerizing blue hues. The Titlis Cliff Walk, Europe’s highest suspension bridge, offers thrilling views, perfect for adventurous travelers.**

## **交通方式 (How to Get There)**  
1. **從琉森出發：** 搭乘火車到恩格爾貝格（Engelberg），車程約1小時。  
2. **纜車接駁：** 從恩格爾貝格乘坐鐵力士纜車系統，經過三段纜車便可到達山頂，最後一段是360度旋轉纜車（Titlis Rotair）。  
3. **自駕：** 從琉森自駕約45分鐘到恩格爾貝格，車站附近設有停車場。

**1. From Lucerne: Take a train to Engelberg, a journey of approximately one hour.  
2. Cable Car Transfer: From Engelberg, ride the Titlis cable car system in three stages to reach the summit, with the final stage being the Titlis Rotair rotating cable car.  
3. By Car: Drive from Lucerne to Engelberg in about 45 minutes, where parking is available near the station.**

## **地理位置 (Geographical Location)**  
鐵力士山位於瑞士中部的恩格爾貝格區，靠近琉森湖，周圍被阿爾卑斯山環繞。該山峰坐落於瑞士和奧地利的分界處，是欣賞阿爾卑斯山壯麗風景的絕佳地點。

**Mount Titlis is located in the Engelberg region of central Switzerland, near Lake Lucerne, and is surrounded by the majestic Alps. It sits at the border between Switzerland and Austria, making it an excellent spot to enjoy the grandeur of the Alpine landscape.**

## **健行路線 (Hiking Routes)**  
1. **冰川步道（Glacier Path）** - 一條平坦的路徑，讓遊客在安全環境下近距離欣賞冰川。  
2. **恩格爾貝格谷健行（Engelberg Valley Trail）** - 從山腳開始，經過森林和草原，最終到達纜車站。  
3. **四湖健行（Four Lakes Hike）** - 途經多個湖泊，風景如畫，是一次令人放鬆的健行體驗。

**1. Glacier Path - A flat trail allowing visitors to admire the glacier up close in a safe environment.  
2. Engelberg Valley Trail - Starts at the base, passing through forests and meadows, leading to the cable car station.  
3. Four Lakes Hike - A scenic route that passes several picturesque lakes, ideal for a relaxing hike.**


# 黃金列車：琉森到因特拉肯 (Golden Pass Line: Lucerne to Interlaken)

## **景點名稱 (Attraction Name)**  
黃金列車（Golden Pass Line）：琉森至因特拉肯路段

## **歷史資訊 (Historical Information)**  
黃金列車是瑞士最著名的觀光火車路線之一，其歷史可以追溯到19世紀末期的瑞士鐵路建設時期。這條線路連接了瑞士中部與西部，展現了阿爾卑斯山區壯麗的自然景色。琉森到因特拉肯這一段的特色在於途經布里恩茨湖和圖恩湖，以及穿越美麗的阿爾卑斯山谷。現代的黃金列車提供全景窗設計，讓旅客能享受極致的觀景體驗。

**The Golden Pass Line is one of Switzerland's most iconic scenic train routes, with its origins dating back to the late 19th century during Switzerland's railway expansion. This route connects central and western Switzerland, showcasing the stunning landscapes of the Alps. The segment between Lucerne and Interlaken is particularly renowned for passing by Lake Brienz and Lake Thun, as well as traversing picturesque Alpine valleys. Modern trains feature panoramic windows for an unparalleled viewing experience.**

## **此處特色 (Unique Aspects)**  
這段旅程的特色包括全景窗列車設計，讓旅客能欣賞阿爾卑斯山的壯麗景色。行程中，列車會穿越許多隧道與橋樑，例如著名的布倫尼山口（Brünig Pass）。途中風景多樣化，包括清澈的湖泊、茂密的森林、以及點綴著傳統瑞士木屋的小鎮。冬季和夏季的景色各有不同，是一年四季都適合的體驗。

**This journey features trains with panoramic windows that allow passengers to enjoy breathtaking views of the Alps. The route crosses numerous tunnels and bridges, including the famous Brünig Pass. The scenery varies along the way, with crystal-clear lakes, dense forests, and charming villages dotted with traditional Swiss chalets. Each season offers a unique experience, making it a year-round attraction.**

## **交通方式 (How to Get There)**  
1. **列車資訊：** 黃金列車從琉森火車站出發，終點為因特拉肯東站（Interlaken Ost）。車程約為2小時。  
2. **班次頻率：** 每日運行，建議提前在瑞士國鐵（SBB）網站或應用程式查詢時刻表並預訂座位。  
3. **票務建議：** 瑞士旅行通票（Swiss Travel Pass）可用於此段列車，對國際旅客十分便利。

**1. Train Information: The Golden Pass Line departs from Lucerne Station and terminates at Interlaken Ost Station, with a journey time of about 2 hours.  
2. Schedule Frequency: Operates daily; it is recommended to check the timetable and reserve seats in advance via the Swiss Federal Railways (SBB) website or app.  
3. Ticketing Tip: The Swiss Travel Pass is valid for this route and is highly convenient for international travelers.**

## **地理位置 (Geographical Location)**  
列車路線橫跨瑞士中部與伯恩高地，經過布倫尼山口，並沿著布里恩茨湖與圖恩湖前行。這條路線展現了瑞士的多樣地貌，從湖泊到山谷，每一段都是自然風光的盛宴。

**The train route stretches across central Switzerland and the Bernese Oberland, passing through the Brünig Pass and along Lake Brienz and Lake Thun. It showcases Switzerland's diverse landscapes, from lakes to valleys, offering a feast of natural beauty at every turn.**

## **健行路線 (Hiking Routes)**  
黃金列車途經許多適合健行的地點，以下是一些推薦路線：  
1. **布倫尼山口步道（Brünig Pass Trail）** - 沿火車路線附近徒步，體驗與列車相似的美景。  
2. **布里恩茨湖健行（Lake Brienz Hike）** - 在布里恩茨湖附近的健行路線，欣賞湖畔景色。  
3. **巴倫貝格露天博物館（Ballenberg Open-Air Museum Hike）** - 可以結合文化探索與自然健行。

**The Golden Pass Line passes several excellent hiking spots. Here are a few recommendations:  
1. Brünig Pass Trail - Hike near the train route to enjoy similar views.  
2. Lake Brienz Hike - Explore trails around Lake Brienz for stunning lakeside scenery.  
3. Ballenberg Open-Air Museum Hike - Combine cultural exploration with nature hiking.**

# 勞特布魯嫩 (Lauterbrunnen)

## **歷史 (History)**  
勞特布魯嫩谷地位於瑞士伯恩高地，是一個以壯麗瀑布聞名的山谷，其名稱意為“許多泉水”。早期的文獻記載可以追溯到13世紀。這個地區以其豐富的自然資源和農業而繁榮，並且是登山活動和阿爾卑斯探險的重要起點。在18世紀浪漫主義時期，這裡的美景吸引了眾多畫家和詩人，包括著名詩人約翰·沃爾夫岡·馮·歌德，他在此創作了詩作《靈感之歌》（Gesang der Geister über den Wassern）。

**The Lauterbrunnen Valley, located in the Bernese Oberland region of Switzerland, is renowned for its stunning waterfalls. Its name translates to "many fountains." Historical records date back to the 13th century. The area thrived on its natural resources and agriculture, becoming a hub for mountaineering and Alpine exploration. During the Romantic era of the 18th century, the valley’s beauty inspired many artists and poets, including Johann Wolfgang von Goethe, who composed his poem "Song of the Spirits over the Waters" here.**

## **地理 (Geography)**  
勞特布魯嫩谷地是阿爾卑斯山最著名的冰蝕谷之一，四周被巍峨的山峰環繞，包括僧侶峰（Mönch）和少女峰（Jungfrau）。谷地長約12公里，以其72條壯觀的瀑布聞名，其中包括著名的施陶河瀑布（Staubbach Falls）。該地區的氣候屬於溫帶山地氣候，夏季涼爽，冬季積雪，是滑雪和健行的理想地點。

**The Lauterbrunnen Valley is one of the most iconic glacial valleys in the Alps, surrounded by towering peaks such as the Mönch and Jungfrau. The valley stretches about 12 kilometers and is famous for its 72 breathtaking waterfalls, including the famous Staubbach Falls. The region has a temperate mountain climate with cool summers and snowy winters, making it ideal for skiing and hiking.**

## **此處特色 (Unique Aspects)**  
勞特布魯嫩的最大特色是其壯麗的自然景觀。施陶河瀑布是歐洲最高的自由落體瀑布之一，其落差達297米。附近的特吕默尔河瀑布（Trümmelbach Falls）隱藏在山內，是世界少有的冰川融水瀑布。除此之外，谷地還是通往少女峰地區和席爾特峰（Schilthorn）的重要起點，吸引了大量探險家和自然愛好者。

**Lauterbrunnen is renowned for its stunning natural scenery. The Staubbach Falls, one of the highest free-falling waterfalls in Europe, plunges 297 meters. Nearby, the Trümmelbach Falls, hidden inside the mountain, is a rare glacial meltwater cascade. The valley also serves as a gateway to the Jungfrau region and Schilthorn, attracting adventurers and nature enthusiasts.**

## **美食 (Cuisine)**  
1. **奶酪火鍋（Cheese Fondue）** - 瑞士經典美食，適合寒冷的日子。  
2. **瑞士馬鈴薯餅（Rösti）** - 傳統土豆料理，搭配熏肉或乳酪更為美味。  
3. **勞特布魯嫩火腿（Lauterbrunnen Ham）** - 當地特色的風乾火腿。  
4. **巧克力甜點（Swiss Chocolate Desserts）** - 瑞士出產的優質巧克力，常用於蛋糕和糕點。  

**1. Cheese Fondue - A Swiss classic, perfect for cold days.  
2. Rösti - A traditional potato dish, often paired with smoked meat or cheese.  
3. Lauterbrunnen Ham - A local specialty of air-dried ham.  
4. Swiss Chocolate Desserts - High-quality Swiss chocolate used in cakes and pastries.**

## **著名的觀光景點 (Famous Attractions)**  
1. **施陶河瀑布（Staubbach Falls）** - 一條壯觀的瀑布，落差接近300米。  
2. **特吕默尔河瀑布（Trümmelbach Falls）** - 可參觀的冰川融水瀑布，藏於山內。  
3. **少女峰（Jungfraujoch）** - 歐洲最高的火車站，被稱為“歐洲之巔”。  
4. **席爾特峰（Schilthorn）** - 提供360度全景，曾是007電影的取景地。  
5. **阿爾門德胡布觀景台（Allmendhubel）** - 透過纜車輕鬆抵達，欣賞山谷全景。  

**1. Staubbach Falls - A spectacular waterfall with a nearly 300-meter drop.  
2. Trümmelbach Falls - Accessible glacial meltwater waterfalls hidden inside the mountain.  
3. Jungfraujoch - Europe’s highest railway station, known as the "Top of Europe."  
4. Schilthorn - Offers 360-degree views and was a filming location for the James Bond movie.  
5. Allmendhubel - A viewpoint accessible via cable car, offering panoramic views of the valley.**

## **健行路線 (Hiking Routes)**  
1. **勞特布魯嫩谷步道（Lauterbrunnen Valley Trail）** - 輕鬆的路徑，經過多條瀑布。  
2. **少女峰小徑（Jungfrau Trail）** - 從勞特布魯嫩前往少女峰地區的絕美路線。  
3. **穆倫步道（Mürren Trail）** - 從勞特布魯嫩到穆倫，沿途可欣賞山谷和阿爾卑斯山景色。  
4. **席爾特峰健行（Schilthorn Hike）** - 更具挑戰性的路線，直通席爾特峰。

**1. Lauterbrunnen Valley Trail - An easy path passing by several waterfalls.  
2. Jungfrau Trail - A stunning route leading from Lauterbrunnen to the Jungfrau region.  
3. Mürren Trail - Offers views of the valley and the Alps, connecting Lauterbrunnen and Mürren.  
4. Schilthorn Hike - A more challenging trail leading to the Schilthorn summit.**


# 格林德瓦 (Grindelwald)

## **歷史 (History)**  
格林德瓦是一個位於瑞士伯恩高地的美麗山村，其歷史可追溯到中世紀時期，最早的記載出現在1146年。該地因其農業和乳製品生產而繁榮。19世紀初，隨著阿爾卑斯探險活動的興起，格林德瓦成為登山愛好者的熱門目的地，尤其是前往艾格峰（Eiger）的登山者。1888年，第一條鐵路連接格林德瓦，進一步促進了旅遊業的發展，並使其成為瑞士最早的國際度假勝地之一。

**Grindelwald is a picturesque mountain village in the Bernese Oberland, Switzerland, with historical records dating back to 1146. It initially thrived on agriculture and dairy production. In the early 19th century, with the rise of Alpine exploration, Grindelwald became a popular destination for climbers, especially those attempting the Eiger. In 1888, the first railway reached Grindelwald, further boosting its tourism and establishing it as one of Switzerland's earliest international resort destinations.**

## **地理 (Geography)**  
格林德瓦位於伯恩高地的山谷中，被壯麗的阿爾卑斯山包圍，尤其是著名的艾格峰（Eiger）、僧侶峰（Mönch）和少女峰（Jungfrau）。該地的海拔約1,034米，四周景色壯麗，擁有多條冰川和溪流。格林德瓦屬於山地氣候，夏季溫和涼爽，冬季積雪豐厚，是滑雪和健行的理想地點。

**Grindelwald is situated in a valley in the Bernese Oberland, surrounded by the majestic Alps, including the iconic Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau peaks. The village sits at an elevation of approximately 1,034 meters, offering breathtaking landscapes with numerous glaciers and streams. It has a mountain climate with mild summers and snowy winters, making it perfect for skiing and hiking.**

## **此處特色 (Unique Aspects)**  
格林德瓦以其阿爾卑斯山的壯麗景色和多樣的戶外活動而聞名。艾格峰北壁（Eiger North Face）是全球登山愛好者的挑戰聖地，而格林德瓦-第一峰（Grindelwald First）則以全景視野和刺激的活動（如高空滑索）吸引旅客。此外，這裡還擁有艾格冰川和高山牧場，是體驗瑞士自然美景的最佳地點之一。

**Grindelwald is renowned for its stunning Alpine scenery and a variety of outdoor activities. The Eiger North Face is a legendary challenge for climbers worldwide, while Grindelwald First is famous for its panoramic views and thrilling attractions like zip-lining. The area also boasts the Eiger Glacier and alpine pastures, making it one of the best places to experience Switzerland's natural beauty.**

## **美食 (Cuisine)**  
1. **瑞士乳酪火鍋（Cheese Fondue）** - 以本地乳酪製成，適合寒冷天氣享用。  
2. **瑞士馬鈴薯餅（Rösti）** - 用土豆煎製而成，搭配當地火腿或乳酪是絕佳選擇。  
3. **山羊奶酪（Goat Cheese）** - 格林德瓦地區特色的奶製品。  
4. **蘋果派（Swiss Apple Tart）** - 傳統甜點，搭配香濃的奶油。  
5. **當地肉類拼盤（Local Meat Platter）** - 包括風乾牛肉、火腿和香腸，配合麵包享用。

**1. Cheese Fondue - Made with local cheese, perfect for cold weather.  
2. Rösti - Fried potato pancakes often paired with local ham or cheese.  
3. Goat Cheese - A regional specialty in Grindelwald.  
4. Swiss Apple Tart - A traditional dessert served with rich cream.  
5. Local Meat Platter - Includes air-dried beef, ham, and sausages, enjoyed with bread.**

## **著名的觀光景點 (Famous Attractions)**  
1. **艾格峰北壁（Eiger North Face）** - 世界著名的登山挑戰地點。  
2. **格林德瓦-第一峰（Grindelwald First）** - 提供高空滑索和懸崖步道等活動。  
3. **艾格冰川（Eiger Glacier）** - 可乘坐齒軌列車抵達，欣賞冰川景觀。  
4. **巴克阿爾普湖（Bachalpsee）** - 一個風景如畫的高山湖泊，適合輕鬆健行。  
5. **纜車觀景（Pfingstegg Cable Car）** - 提供俯瞰山谷的壯麗視野。  
6. **少女峰火車之旅（Jungfraujoch Train Journey）** - 從格林德瓦出發，前往歐洲之巔。  

**1. Eiger North Face - A world-renowned climbing destination.  
2. Grindelwald First - Offers activities like zip-lining and cliff walks.  
3. Eiger Glacier - Accessible by cogwheel train, showcasing spectacular glacier views.  
4. Bachalpsee - A picturesque alpine lake perfect for easy hikes.  
5. Pfingstegg Cable Car - Provides stunning views of the valley.  
6. Jungfraujoch Train Journey - From Grindelwald to the Top of Europe.**

## **健行路線 (Hiking Routes)**  
1. **巴克阿爾普湖步道（Bachalpsee Trail）** - 輕鬆路徑，適合家庭徒步，沿途欣賞湖光山色。  
2. **艾格小徑（Eiger Trail）** - 沿著艾格峰北壁，適合中等難度的登山者。  
3. **格林德瓦-第一峰懸崖步道（First Cliff Walk）** - 風景壯麗且具有挑戰性的步道。  
4. **皮申斯特格步道（Pfingstegg Trail）** - 通往俯瞰格林德瓦谷的高山地點。  
5. **少女峰健行（Jungfrau Hiking Trails）** - 通往少女峰地區的多條路線，風景迷人。  

**1. Bachalpsee Trail - An easy route suitable for families, with stunning lake and mountain views.  
2. Eiger Trail - Runs along the Eiger North Face, ideal for moderate-level hikers.  
3. First Cliff Walk - A scenic and challenging trail.  
4. Pfingstegg Trail - Leads to a high vantage point overlooking the Grindelwald Valley.  
5. Jungfrau Hiking Trails - Multiple routes to the Jungfrau region with breathtaking scenery.**
